The R Project for Statistical Computing

R is a language and environment for statistical computing, graphics and data analysis. R provides a wide variety of statistical (linear and nonlinear modelling, classical statistical tests, time-series analysis, classification, clustering, …) and graphical techniques, and is highly extensible. R is available as Free Software under the terms of the GNU General Public License v2 in source code form. It compiles and runs on a wide variety platforms including Windows, Mac, and Linux.
